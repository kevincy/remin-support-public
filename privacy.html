Privacy Policy隐私政策

Effective July 20, 2026 · Last updated July 20, 2026生效日期：2026 年 7 月 20 日 · 最后更新：2026 年 7 月 20 日

Short version: Remin Cards is local-first. Your decks and study history stay on your device unless you choose to sync them through your private iCloud account. We do not sell personal data, run advertising trackers, or require a Remin account.

1. Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains how the developer of Remin Cards (“Remin Cards,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) handles information when you use the Remin Cards app, its Mac Catalyst version, and this support site.

2. Information processed by the app

Study content and app data

Remin Cards processes the content you create or import, including deck and Folder names, card fields, tags, templates, images, audio, scheduling information, review history, study plans, preferences, and import history. This information is stored locally in the app’s private container on your device.

We do not operate a proprietary account server that receives this content.

Optional iCloud sync

If you choose iCloud sync, Remin Cards sends the app data needed for synchronization to the app’s private CloudKit database in your Apple iCloud account. Apple operates iCloud and processes this data under Apple’s terms and privacy policy. Your iCloud account and available storage are managed by Apple.

The app uses the private CloudKit database, not a public shared database. The developer does not routinely receive or browse the contents of your private iCloud database. You can disable iCloud access or remove stored iCloud data through Apple’s device and iCloud settings.

Photos, camera, and microphone

  • Photos: accessed only when you select an image to add to a card.
  • Camera: requested only when you choose a feature that captures a handout, recognizes text on-device, or creates card media.
  • Microphone: requested only when you choose to record pronunciation, an explanation, or another audio attachment.

Selected or recorded media becomes part of your local card content and, if you enable iCloud sync, may also be included in your private iCloud data. Remin Cards does not activate the camera or microphone at launch.

In-app purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment-card details. The app receives product and transaction status needed to unlock, restore, refund, or revoke Remin Cards Pro access. Apple separately handles purchase and account information under its own privacy policy.

Diagnostics

Remin Cards does not include third-party advertising or behavioral analytics SDKs. If you choose to share diagnostics with app developers in your Apple device settings, Apple may make crash or performance reports available to us. Those reports are used only to improve reliability and are subject to Apple’s controls.

3. Imported templates and external resources

Imported card templates may contain HTML, CSS, JavaScript, links, or images hosted by third parties. Remin Cards restricts template capabilities, but an external image may be requested when a card is displayed, or a link may open after you select it. In those cases, the third-party host may receive technical information such as your IP address and browser request details under its own privacy policy.

Review imported packages before using them, especially packages from an unknown source. For maximum privacy and offline reliability, use media included inside the package rather than remotely hosted resources.

4. How information is used

Information processed by Remin Cards is used to:

  • display, edit, schedule, search, and export your cards;
  • calculate FSRS schedules and learning statistics;
  • sync your data between devices when requested;
  • verify Pro access and restore purchases;
  • diagnose failures when you voluntarily send a support report.

We do not sell your personal information and do not use it for cross-app tracking or targeted advertising.

5. Sharing

We do not share your study content with data brokers or advertisers. Data may be processed by Apple when you use iCloud, StoreKit, App Store distribution, or Apple diagnostics. Information may also be disclosed if required by applicable law, or when you explicitly choose to export or share a file with another app or person.

6. Retention and deletion

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete content in the app, clear the app’s data, or uninstall the app.
  • Deleting the app normally removes its local container, but does not necessarily remove data already stored in iCloud.
  • Private iCloud data remains subject to your Apple account and iCloud settings. You can manage or delete it through Apple’s settings.
  • Apple retains purchase records as required to operate the App Store and comply with law.
  • Support emails and issue reports are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to resolve the request, prevent abuse, and meet legal obligations.

Export a copy of any data you want to keep before deleting the app or its iCloud data.

7. Security

Remin Cards uses Apple platform protections, app sandboxing, local storage, and CloudKit transport protections. No system can guarantee absolute security. Protect your device passcode and Apple Account, keep your software updated, and import packages only from sources you trust.

8. Children

Remin Cards is a general education tool and is not designed to collect personal information from children. We do not knowingly operate accounts or advertising profiles for children. A parent or guardian should supervise a child’s use where required by local law.

9. International use

Apple may process iCloud, purchase, and diagnostic information in locations described in Apple’s privacy documentation. Support messages you send to us may be processed where the developer operates.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when features, legal requirements, or data practices change. We will update the date above and, when appropriate, provide notice in the app or on this site.

简要说明：Remin Cards 采用本地优先设计。你的牌组和学习历史默认保存在设备上；只有当你主动选择同步时，数据才会进入你自己的私人 iCloud。我们不出售个人数据、不使用广告追踪，也不要求注册 Remin 账户。

1. 适用范围

本隐私政策说明 Remin Cards 的开发者（以下称“Remin Cards”“我们”）在你使用 Remin Cards iOS App、Mac Catalyst 版本及本支持网站时如何处理信息。

2. App 处理的信息

学习内容和 App 数据

Remin Cards 会处理你创建或导入的内容，包括牌组与“我的分组”名称、卡片字段、标签、模板、图片、音频、调度信息、复习历史、学习计划、偏好设置和导入历史。这些信息保存在设备上 App 的私有容器内。

我们没有用于接收上述学习内容的自建账户服务器。

可选的 iCloud 同步

当你选择 iCloud 同步时，Remin Cards 会将同步所需的 App 数据发送到你 Apple iCloud 账户中属于本 App 的私人 CloudKit 数据库。iCloud 由 Apple 运营，Apple 根据其条款和隐私政策处理相关数据；iCloud 账户和存储空间由 Apple 管理。

App 使用私人 CloudKit 数据库，而不是公开共享数据库。开发者通常无法接收或浏览你私人 iCloud 数据库中的内容。你可以在 Apple 设备及 iCloud 设置中停用 App 的 iCloud 权限或删除相应的 iCloud 数据。

照片、相机和麦克风

  • 照片：仅当你主动选择图片添加到卡片时访问。
  • 相机：仅当你选择拍摄资料、在设备上识别文字或创建卡片媒体时请求。
  • 麦克风：仅当你选择录制发音、讲解或其他音频附件时请求。

选取或录制的媒体会成为你的本地卡片内容；若开启 iCloud 同步，也可能包含在你的私人 iCloud 数据中。Remin Cards 不会在启动时自动开启相机或麦克风。

App 内购买

购买由 Apple 通过 StoreKit 处理。我们不会收到完整的银行卡资料。App 会接收解锁、恢复、退款或撤销 Remin Cards Pro 权益所需的产品和交易状态。购买及 Apple 账户信息由 Apple 根据其隐私政策另行处理。

诊断信息

Remin Cards 不包含第三方广告或行为分析 SDK。若你在 Apple 设备设置中选择与开发者共享诊断数据，Apple 可能向我们提供崩溃或性能报告。这些报告仅用于提高可靠性，并受 Apple 的相关设置控制。

3. 导入模板和外部资源

导入的卡片模板可能包含 HTML、CSS、JavaScript、链接或第三方托管的图片。Remin Cards 会限制模板能力，但卡片显示时可能请求外部图片，或在你点击后打开链接。此时第三方服务可能依据其隐私政策收到 IP 地址和请求信息等技术数据。

请检查导入的牌组，尤其是来源不明的文件。为了获得更好的隐私和离线可靠性，建议使用打包在 .apkg 内的媒体，而不是远程资源。

4. 信息用途

Remin Cards 处理信息的用途包括：

  • 显示、编辑、调度、搜索和导出卡片；
  • 计算 FSRS 调度和学习统计；
  • 按你的要求在设备之间同步；
  • 验证 Pro 权益和恢复购买；
  • 在你主动发送支持报告时诊断问题。

我们不出售个人信息，也不会将其用于跨 App 追踪或定向广告。

5. 信息共享

我们不会将学习内容提供给数据经纪商或广告商。使用 iCloud、StoreKit、App Store 分发或 Apple 诊断时，Apple 可能处理相关数据。法律要求时，或当你主动导出并与其他 App 或人员分享文件时，信息也可能被披露。

6. 保留与删除

  • 本地数据会保留在设备上，直到你在 App 中删除内容、清除 App 数据或卸载 App。
  • 卸载 App 通常会删除其本地容器，但不一定会删除已经保存在 iCloud 中的数据。
  • 私人 iCloud 数据受 Apple 账户及 iCloud 设置管理，你可以通过 Apple 设置进行管理或删除。
  • Apple 会根据运营 App Store 和法律合规需要保留购买记录。
  • 支持邮件和问题报告仅在解决问题、防止滥用及履行法律义务所需的合理期限内保留。

删除 App 或 iCloud 数据前，请先导出你希望保留的内容。

7. 安全

Remin Cards 使用 Apple 平台安全机制、App 沙盒、本地存储和 CloudKit 传输保护。任何系统都无法保证绝对安全。请保护好设备密码和 Apple 账户、及时更新软件，并只导入可信来源的文件。

8. 儿童隐私

Remin Cards 是通用教育工具，不以收集儿童个人信息为目的。我们不会故意为儿童建立账户或广告画像。当地法律要求时，儿童应在父母或监护人指导下使用。

9. 跨境处理

Apple 可能在其隐私文件说明的地区处理 iCloud、购买和诊断信息。你主动发送的支持消息可能在开发者所在地处理。

10. 政策变更

功能、法律要求或数据处理方式发生变化时，我们可能更新本政策。我们会更新页面顶部日期，并在适当情况下通过 App 或本网站通知。
